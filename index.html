Christmas Sugar Cookies

A Sweet Christmas treat

By Lisa MacLean


Ingredients


  • Butter
  • Confectioners' sugar
  • Sour Cream
  • Honey
  • Baking soda
  • Cram of tartar
  • Mace
  • White candy coating

Directions


    Estimated total time: 3-4 hours


  1. Using a stand mixer or hand mixer, cream the butter and sugar in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg, then the sour cream, honey and vanilla. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking sida, cream of tartar, mace and salt. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, and beat well. Cover the dough, and chill for two hours or until easy to handle.

    2. Estimated time: 2 hours


  2. Preheat the oven to 325°F. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to 1/8-inch thickness.Cut out cookies using floured 3-inch cookie cutters. Place the cookies 1 inch apart on ungreased baking sheets.

    3. Estimated time: 30 minutes


  3. Bake the cookies until lightly browned, 8 to 10 minutes. Remove the cookies to wire racks to cool.

    4. Estimated time: 15 minutes


  4. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the white coating, and stir until smooth. Stir in food coloring,if using, and drizzle over the cookies.

    5. Estimated time: 30 minutes
